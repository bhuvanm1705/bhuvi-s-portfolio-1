Foru.ms - Redefining Community

Foru.ms is a Next.js & Firebase powered community platform that redefines the forum experience. It leverages 'Creative Misuse' to transform standard discussion threads into dynamic Job Boards, Collaborative Stories, and Product Roadmaps.

This project emphasizes flexibility and user-driven structure, allowing communities to mold the platform to their specific needs.
